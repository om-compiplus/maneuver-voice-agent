## Services
Maneuver helps climate and mobility startups with product strategy, rapid prototyping, user research, and go-to-market planning. We specialize in:
- Product vision and positioning
- UX design and service design
- Low-latency MVP development
- Growth experimentation and acquisition funnels

## Process
Our process is founder-friendly and built for speed. We start with a discovery sprint to align on customer problems, then move into a rapid prototyping cycle:
1. Discovery and alignment
2. Prototype and user feedback
3. Iteration and launch readiness
4. Growth and measurement

We keep communication direct, share working prototypes each week, and update the plan as we learn.

## Pricing
Maneuver typically engages on scoped projects with clear outcomes. We recommend:
- Discovery sprint: 2–4 weeks
- Core product build: 8–12 weeks
- Ongoing growth support: monthly retainer

We can work with fixed-scope budgets or a lightweight retainer depending on the project.

## Team
The founder-led team combines product strategy, design, and engineering. I personally lead discovery, roadmap decisions, and customer interviews, while a small experienced delivery team executes the product build.

## Case Studies
We have delivered early-stage products for climate and mobility companies, including an EV charging network experience, a freight visibility dashboard, and a subscription product for carbon credits.

## Positioning
Maneuver is for startups that value clarity over complexity. We help founders move from idea to usable product quickly, with a focus on customer outcomes and measurable momentum.
